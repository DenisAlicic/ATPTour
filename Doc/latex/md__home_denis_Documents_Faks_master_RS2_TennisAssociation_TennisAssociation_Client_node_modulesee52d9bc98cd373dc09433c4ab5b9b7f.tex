A robust Punycode converter that fully complies to \href{http://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc3492}{\texttt{ RFC 3492}} and \href{http://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc5891}{\texttt{ RFC 5891}}, and works on nearly all Java\+Script platforms.

This Java\+Script library is the result of comparing, optimizing and documenting different open-\/source implementations of the Punycode algorithm\+:
